Translate Bubble

Translations without interruptions

Available on Google Play
This project is made with Scala for Android & Love. Handcrafted by 47 Degrees and is OpenSource

1. Copy to clipboard

Select the text you want to translate and copy it to the clipboard

2. Bubble appears

A bubble containing the translation appear and will not interrupt your current task

3. Click the bubble

You'll see the translation. You can move the results around on your screen

4. Use with Android Wear

Have a Android Wear watch? Enable notifications and your translations appear directly on your wrist!
