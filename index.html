Translate Bubble

Translations without interruptions

Available on Google Play
This Open Source project is handcrafted by 47 Degrees and written entirely in Scala on Android.

1. Copy to clipboard

Select the text you want to translate and copy it to the clipboard.

2. Bubble appears

A bubble containing the translation will appear and will not interrupt your current task.

3. Click the bubble

You'll see the translation. You can move the results around on your screen.

4. Use with Android Wear

Have a Android Wear watch? Enable notifications and your translations will appear directly on your wrist!
